Attractions

Nitro Arm

Nitro the AI powered robot arm has malfunctioned. Can you stand up to the challenge and outlast Nitro's battery?

Ferris Wheel

Family fun and beautiful views. Ride the tallest ferris wheel this side of the Misty Mountains. Enjoy the beautiful view from the top with your family.

The Ludicrous Speedster

The world's fastest rollercoaster where lightspeed is too slow, other rollercoasters will feel like you're riding in a Cuisinart®. You better buckle up!

Accomodations

Hotel Arthur Dent

Relax and bask in the sun by one of the 6 luxury pools. Hotel Guests can dine in one of the 4 fine dining restaurants or take a 5 minute walk to Wally World at their convenience.

Dining

Raymond's Cafe

Picky eaters? With an extensive menu everyone in your group will find something to eat at Raymond's Cafe.

Jiro Dreams of Sushi

Enjoy fresh cuts of sushi and sashimi while taking a break from the rides.

The In Guys Shack

Treat the whole family with a quick meal of hamburgers and fries. Try the veggie burger and oven baked fries for an alternate healthy meal.
